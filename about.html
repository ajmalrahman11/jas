ABOUT US

Jagadamba Auto Solutions is a genuine TVS 2-wheeler spare parts distributor in Tamil Nadu, India. It was founded in Salem, Tamil Nadu. The promoters of Jagadamba Auto Solutions have more than 35 years of experience in the 2 and 3 aftermarket spare parts industry. Evaluating our performance and growth, in 2017, TVS appointed a ‘Hub n Spoke’ distributor in Tiruchirapalli. We have a wide network of over 1200 B2B customers. Customer satisfaction is our motto since we are the only source of TVS parts, it is our responsibility to supply the parts within 12-24 hours with an extensive range of approximately 6500 SKUs.
