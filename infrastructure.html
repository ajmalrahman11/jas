INFRASTRUCTURE

Our State of Art Facilities

WAREHOUSES

We ensure that sufficient warehouse spacing is given for smooth operations. Every space within the warehouse is earmarked for different tasks such as Loading / Unloading areas: These areas are used for temporary storage of packages being loaded and unloaded from / to the warehouse. Storage racks have been installed in close proximity of delivery vehicle which ensures greater load handling speed.

STORAGE AREA

The location on any part in the warehouse can be easily identified as all the bin and rack numbers are correctly labelled, displayed, and recorded in our in-house ERP software. This has proven to decrease the amount of time it takes to identify the location of a part. The store operator would enter the part number on the system to identify its location and follow the correct signs.

WORK STATIONS

Each office associate whose tasks ranges from sales, purchase, and accounts have been assigned separate workstations which have systems installed.

DISPATCH AREA

These areas are used for packing orders picked up from the storage area. The sticker machine and seal machine are installed in this area which reduces the time required to pack orders.

PANTRY AREA

The pantry area is equipped with tea / coffee machines, microwaves and sitting space for employees.

SERVICE AREA

A small part of the warehouse is assigned to support activities at the facility, such as changing rooms, and washrooms.
